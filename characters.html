CHARACTERS

Jesus, the Son of Jehovah, exemplified perfect obedience and love. His life of sacrifice and teaching provides the ultimate example for all.

For I have given you an example, that you should do just as I have done to you. — John 13:15

This is my Son, the beloved, whom I have approved. — Matthew 3:17

Abraham’s faith and obedience made him a friend of Jehovah, trusting in His promises despite great trials.

By faith Abraham, when he was tested, as good as offered up Isaac. — Hebrews 11:17

David, a man after God’s own heart, showed courage and repentance, trusting Jehovah in all circumstances.

Jehovah is my shepherd; I will lack nothing. — Psalm 23:1
